about us

Krakatoa Coffee Company is a black-owned coffee company that sells a variety of fresh, roasted to order coffee to satisfy every coffee drinkers palate. Our coffee comes in standard (ground), espresso, whole bean, and single serve cups. We also sell mugs, in case you need something to drink our delicious coffee from, and we offer gift cards in several different denominations.
